A Buyer’s Guide to PEO Services

Written by Deel Team | Apr 18, 2024 5:55:39 PM

Whether you’re setting up shop in the US or scaling across new states, managing employment from payroll to compliance is no small feat. It’s complex, it’s time-consuming, and it’s different in every jurisdiction. And as your business expands, managing HR and compliance with local laws increases exponentially. 

As a result, HR and Finance teams are often overloaded with regulatory compliance and administrative overhead burdens.

With the right PEO, you can alleviate some of these burdens by offloading payroll, compliance, HR functions, and benefits administration, allowing your team to focus on core business functions and strategic growth.

Our Buyer’s Guide to PEO Services breaks down everything you need to know to find the best solution for your team, whether you're switching providers or starting your search from scratch:

  • Key features and functions: Learn what essential services a PEO provides, including sophisticated technology platforms and comprehensive HR services
  • PEO evaluation checklist: Our checklist helps you evaluate potential PEOs on critical aspects such as payroll administration, technology integration, HR services, access controls, and risk management
  • Benefits of partnering with a PEO: Discover how the right PEO can transform your operational efficiency through better compliance, scalability, and reduced administrative costs
