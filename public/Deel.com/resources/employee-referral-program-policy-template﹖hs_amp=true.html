Employee referral program policy template overview

An employee referral program policy is a document that outlines a company’s guidelines regarding potential candidate referrals by the organization’s current employees.

The policy includes:

  • General guidelines regarding employee eligibility
  • Referral award description
  • Employee referral forms and procedures
  • Best practices for employees referring new hires
  • Tips on successful employee referral program implementation

How to use this template

This template is a customizable document. You’ll see placeholder text and prompts to guide your policy to reflect your local labor laws and your team’s unique approach to remote work.

We suggest including this policy in your remote workers’ onboarding process to ensure they review and understand all details.
