Free Long-Term International Assignment Policy Template

Written by Deel Team | May 31, 2024 5:11:15 PM

Who will benefit from this policy template?

This policy template will benefit global businesses and their HR departments by creating a clear and comprehensive process for workers undertaking a long-term international assignment. 

International assignments play a crucial role in the success of global companies. The swift, compliant, and cost-effective deployment of team members from their home locations across borders, with minimal disruption, is key to a business’s ongoing expansion and growth.

Policy overview

Long-term international assignment policies are comprehensive frameworks designed to manage various aspects of assigning team members to work in foreign locations for extended periods of one to five years. 

This policy template includes several key components to ensure the assignments are effective, compliant with laws, and beneficial for both the worker and the organization. Here’s what the policy covers:

  • Policy overview
  • Eligibility
  • Work arrangement terms
  • Immigration support
  • Compensation adjustments
  • Relocation support
  • Taxation
  • Extensions
  • Repatriation
