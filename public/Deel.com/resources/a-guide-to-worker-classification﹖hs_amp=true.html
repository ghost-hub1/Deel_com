Who will benefit from this guide?

This guide will benefit small business owners, C-suit decision-makers, and HR and legal personnel seeking practical methods and cutting-edge tools to classify workers worldwide and maintain a globally compliant and thriving workforce.

Guide overview

Global hiring presents exciting growth opportunities, but this expansion introduces complex compliance challenges.

Each country has its own unique set of labor laws and regulations governing the classification of workers, which means companies should avoid a one-size-fits-all approach.

This guide shares practical methods, cutting-edge tools, and robust strategies to help you maintain a globally compliant and thriving workforce.
