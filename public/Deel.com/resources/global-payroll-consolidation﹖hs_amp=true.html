The Case for Global Payroll Consolidation

Written by Deel Team | Dec 19, 2022 9:34:50 PM

Global expansion is an exciting time for any team. There are more markets to explore, more talented workers to engage, and more opportunities for growth.

But if you’re using several providers to pay your international team, there are also more opportunities for security risks, human error, and poor payment experiences. 

By consolidating your global payroll into one superior platform, you can:

  • Increase data visibility
  • Minimize human error
  • Reduce global workforce costs
  • Make well-informed business decisions

And that’s just the beginning. This guide will help you weigh the pros and cons of payroll consolidation to determine if it’s the right step for your team.

It also features real-world examples of how companies like Change.org have used global payroll to save more than 300 hours of admin work each month. Download the resource today to learn more.
