The Business Case for Global Hiring

Written by Deel Team | Aug 24, 2022 7:03:47 PM

Global hiring enables companies to expand their talent pool and find top talent without geographic restrictions.

As you’ll read in this resource, global hiring can positively impact your entire enterprise, from your employee retention and company culture to your market share and profitability.

As a Head of Talent or HR, you see the benefits of expanding your recruitment efforts worldwide. But to build your business case, you need data, real-world examples, and cost comparisons to prove a positive return on investment.

This resource provides Heads of Talent Acquisition and Human Resources with data-driven insights into the benefits of global hiring, empowering them with the information they need to present new hiring initiatives to stakeholders and c-suite executives.
