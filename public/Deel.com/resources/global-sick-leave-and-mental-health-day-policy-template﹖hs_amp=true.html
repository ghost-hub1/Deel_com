Global Sick Leave and Mental Health Policy Overview

A global sick leave policy is a document that outlines a company’s conditions for remote and international workers who become unwell and cannot work.

The policy includes:

  • The company’s method of providing sick leave
  • The procedure and conditions for taking sick leave
  • Conditions surrounding extended leave or leave of absence
  • Conditions surrounding disability benefits
  • The company’s method of providing a mental health day
  • The procedure and conditions for taking a mental health day

How to use this template

This template is a customizable document. You’ll see placeholder text and prompts to guide your policy to reflect your local labor laws and your team’s unique approach to remote work.

We suggest including this policy in your remote workers’ onboarding process to ensure they review and understand the details of the policy.
