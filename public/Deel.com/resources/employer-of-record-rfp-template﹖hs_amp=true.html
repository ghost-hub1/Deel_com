How does a good RFP help your company?

A thorough RFP incentivizes service providers to put together customized, appealing packages for your needs. An RFP process gives you many quality options and reduces the chances of choosing the wrong vendor. You’ll also be able to make your decision faster because you’ll have a thorough proposal addressing all your questions.

Before you request proposals, you’ll need to look deep into your why’s and how’s and think about your business needs. This Request for Proposal template guides you through identifying the features that matter the most to your company.

What does an RFP typically include?

A RFP usually includes:

  • Budget
  • Scope of work
  • Project timeline
  • Project goals
  • Evaluation criteria
