The Power of Global Payroll: How to Streamline International Operations and Ongoing Admin

On-demand

Event Details

When embarking on global expansion, hiring and paying international employees becomes more complex. You must comply with a new set of labour laws each time you hire an employee from a different country. To save time and drive efficiencies, organisations are turning to Global Payroll with Deel to leverage the power of automation and technology. 

Join us for a discussion on how HR and payroll teams are using Global Payroll to store payment data in one place, reduce operational costs and human error, automate payroll, and keep up with country legislation changes. 

We'll cover:

  • Example use cases and early wins for your team
  • How to simplify HR and payroll processes with one consolidated platform
  • A demonstration of Global Payroll
  • Considerations and approaches for introducing automation technology to your HR and payroll team
